Fractional shares

Content Strategy at Robinhood

My contributions

I developed the content strategy for this feature, which entailed naming and positioning the product, writing product (UX) copy, strategizing announcement communications, and developing customer support materials.

Project overview

Fractional Shares allow users to invest in pieces of whole shares. We were the first major brokerage to release this feature, enabling people to buy stocks or ETFs that they might not otherwise be able to afford.
